The house

House Notes

Short, useful reminders for a comfortable stay.

House rules

  • No parties, events, or unregistered overnight guests.
  • Please keep food out of the bedrooms and beds.
  • Pets are welcome when house broken, with a maximum of two pets.
  • Please pick up after pets and keep pet food sealed.
  • Only toilet paper goes in the toilets. Septic systems clog easily.
  • Please do not use kiddie pools, blow-up pools, or anything that heavily draws on the well.
  • The pond is for looking, not swimming. It is home to koi and is not safe for people or pets.
  • Please be respectful of the golf course next door. Children should not go on the course unattended.

Bedrooms and bathrooms

Bedroom 1

King bed. Open loft-style primary bedroom overlooking the living room, with adjacent full bathroom, jetted tub, and waterfall shower.

Bedroom 2

Queen bed on the main floor with an attached full bathroom.

Bedroom 3

Queen bed downstairs with an attached full bathroom.

Bedroom 4

Queen bed plus a bunk bed downstairs, with an attached full bathroom.

Extra blankets are usually in closets, dressers, and baskets in the game room.

Kitchen, dining, and laundry

  • The kitchen is fully equipped, including cookware, serving pieces, spices, oil, coffee, and tea.
  • Dishwasher pods are under the sink. Please keep wooden items out of the dishwasher.
  • The dining table seats about 10. Game room stools can be used for extra seating.
  • Grill utensils are stored on the back side of the kitchen island near the refrigerator end.
  • The washer and dryer are between the kitchen and main floor bathroom. Please empty the lint trap after each use.

Water, Wi-Fi, and TV

  • The small tap on the right side of the kitchen sink provides filtered drinking water. It may look cloudy for a few seconds because of oxygen from the well.
  • A five-gallon water dispenser is also available in the kitchen. Refills can be made at the Catskill Walmart.
  • Wi-Fi details are provided in the home and in your Airbnb arrival information.
  • There are TVs in the living room, primary bedroom, and downstairs game room, with streaming options available.

Heat, cooling, fireplace, and grill

  • Only one thermostat controls forced-air heating and cooling. The other thermostats control radiant floor heat.
  • The lower level has radiant heat only.
  • If it is below 60 degrees outside, please do not run the AC. Open windows instead.
  • The indoor gas fireplace is seasonal, usually mid-October through April. Open the damper before use and leave only the pilot light on before bed.
  • Use the outdoor firepits carefully, keep fires contained, and fully extinguish them when done.
  • Please turn off the propane tank after grilling.

Game room and extras

The game room has a pool table, ping pong table, and air hockey. Beach towels, outdoor blankets, a yoga mat, books, a Pack 'n Play, a high chair, an air mattress, and room dividers are kept around the house, usually in closets or basement storage benches.

Before you leave

Checkout Checklist

Please do

  • Start the dishwasher or hand wash dishes.
  • Take perishable food you brought with you.
  • Put trash in the outdoor bins near the shed.
  • Turn off the propane tank if you used the grill.
  • Turn off lights, close doors, and lock the front door.

No need to do

  • No need to strip beds.
  • No need to gather towels.
  • No need to over-clean. We just ask that the home is left tidy.

Please do not leave trash bags on the porch. Local raccoons are very motivated.

Outside

Hikes, Water, and Day Trips

Bring water, check weather, and use designated parking only.

Easy walks and local hikes

  • Mawignack Preserve - a one-mile loop at 52 Snake Road with creek views, interpretive signs, and leashed-dog walking.
  • Thomas Cole National Historic Site - historic house, gardens, Catskill Mountain views, and grounds open dawn to dusk.
  • Hudson River Skywalk - scenic bridge walk connecting the Thomas Cole and Olana landscapes.
  • Olana State Historic Site - Frederic Church's home, carriage roads, views, and year-round grounds.

Bigger hikes

  • Kaaterskill Falls - iconic waterfall. Park at Laurel House Road, Scutt Road, or South Lake. Do not park illegally on Route 23A or Laurel House Road.
  • Diamond Notch Falls - streamside trail and waterfall. A good mountain day with a moderate effort.
  • Black Dome - challenging hike with steep sections and rewarding views. Best for prepared hikers.

Kaaterskill can be icy, crowded, and slippery. Proper shoes matter, and swimming near falls is dangerous.

Swimming and lake days

  • North-South Lake - beaches, paddling, picnic areas, hiking, and seasonal day-use access. Swimming is at designated beaches only.
  • Colgate Lake - quiet mountain lake, picnic feel, and nearby trails.
  • Lake Taghkanic State Park - beach, playgrounds, picnic areas, and a pool in season.
  • Copake Lake and Bash Bish Falls area - a longer drive, good for a full day out.

Family and rainy-day ideas

  • Catskill Mountain Railroad, Kingston - seasonal scenic train rides, including fall, pumpkin, and holiday rides.
  • Opus 40, Saugerties - outdoor sculpture park, quarry trails, gallery, and picnic-friendly grounds.
  • Saugerties Lighthouse - half-mile nature trail to a river lighthouse. Check tide conditions before visiting.
  • Zoom Flume Water Park - summer family day trip about 30 minutes away.
  • Rail Explorers, Phoenicia - pedal-powered rail carts, about an hour away.
  • Hudson antiques - Warren Street shops, galleries, cafes, and browsing.

Useful to know

Practical Notes

Trash and recycling

Outdoor bins are near the shed. Pickup is typically Wednesday, with bins placed near the top of the driveway.

Ticks and outdoors

Ticks are common in the area. Check yourself, children, and pets after walking through grass, woods, or brush.

Supplies nearby

Walmart, Home Depot, and Lowe's are all close by for forgotten basics, hardware, outdoor gear, or pet supplies.

Medical and pharmacy

Price Chopper and Hannaford have pharmacies nearby. For urgent medical needs, search current local urgent care availability or use emergency services.

Pets

Catskill Animal Hospital is a nearby veterinary option for routine needs. For after-hours emergencies, call ahead to confirm the closest open emergency vet.
